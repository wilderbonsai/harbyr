Manage storage with ease

Your creations kept safe, secure, indivudally managed.

Our network of logistics centers in Germany and Austria are equipped with modern shelving systems and pallet spaces to house your collection of artwork, whether it be a painting, scultpure, installation, or anything else.

We safely store all your artwork in your nearest logistics center, individually managed, ready to dispatch worldwide at your command.

Packed with quality and care

A team of trained handlers and industry standard materials at your fingertips.

Once you are ready to ship a painting somewhere, our skilled packing team manages, and indivudally wraps and packs your artwork to ensure a safe voyage.

Our materials include bubblewrap, cartonage, foam padding, and others. We can also accomodate special needs, or materials on request for your presonal requirements.

Worldwide Delivery

Immediate delivery to your exhibition, to a collector, back to yourself, or anywhere in the world. Hassle free.

As soon as you desire, we immediately start processing the delivery and send it to it's destination on the same day at the best conditions possible.

We compare the shipping rates of trusted shipping service providers and find the best one for you, and take care of customs clearance and any other related paperwork so you don't have to.

How it works.

One simple step at a time.

  • 1
    Send in your works to us

    Once you are finished with a work, and ready for it to be managed, you can ship or drop off your work in one of our nearest managing facilities, where we will carefully, and individually store it away in one of our modern shelving systems or pallet spaces.

  • 2
    Dispatch your works on demand

    Once you're ready to send our your work, whether it be to a gallery, or a collector, we carefully and professionally package and ship it out the same day of your request, without any effort on your part.

  • 3
    Receive and rejoice

    We enable you the status of your delivery and take advantage of real-time updates and track your shipments from order to delivery. We notify you on successful delivery, allowing you to spend less time worrying, and more time celebrating.
