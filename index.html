How it works

One simple step at a time.

  • 1
    Pack your things

    Pack everything you want up, and we handle the rest. You schedule a pickup time, and we pick it up. We bring it to our storage facility where it will be individually managed for you.

  • 2
    Sell or dispatch your items on command

    We individually manage all your things so you can sell anything in your storage when you want, anywhere in the world. We genrate you an amazing webshop to sell your things, and a customer portal where you can individually manage items with single click.

    Receive and rejoice
